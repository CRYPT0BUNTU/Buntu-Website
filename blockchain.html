Blockchain

BNTU


In addition to NFTs, we've created BNTU, a digital currency, for use in our play-to-earn games and AR/VR applications. You can also use BNTU to buy certain NFTs. BNTU can be bought or sold on our participating exchanges listed at the bottom of this page.

BNTU is a peer-to-peer electronic cash system consisting of a distributed ledger (blockchain), coins (Ticker: BNTU), and a network of nodes.

Buntu's proof-of-stake system incentivizes users to run nodes by paying out coins as a reward. The system also uses special nodes known as masternodes to perform functions like facilitating instant transactions. Like staking, masternode operators are periodically rewarded with coins. For additional details, see our whitepaper.
